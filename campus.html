Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin

Kampus Besut, Tembila

UniSZA Kampus Besut is the newest and largest campus of Universiti Sultan Zainal Abidin, operating since 2013 across 956 acres of peaceful natural landscape in Besut, Terengganu. It hosts three faculties, including Fakulti Informatik dan Komputeran (FIK), plus the Central Laboratory Management Centre (CLMC).

I am pursuing Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Development) with Honours under the School of Computer Science at FIK. This page shows my real study spaces, labs, and campus life, alongside official UniSZA resources for visitors.

Fakulti Informatik dan Komputeran (FIK)

Faculty of Informatics and Computing: teaching, research, and community service since 2006.

Three Schools

School of Computer Science · School of Information Technology · School of Multimedia, covering software development, networking, informatics media, and more.

My Programme

Bachelor of Computer Science (Software Development) with Honours, covering web systems, programming, databases, software engineering, and project-based learning.

UniSZA Digital Hub

FIK's research and innovation arm. ICT services, professional training, co-working space, lab booking, and commercialization support for students and industry.
